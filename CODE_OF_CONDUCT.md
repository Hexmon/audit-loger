# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for everyone.

## Be Respectful
Treat others with respect and kindness. Harassment and discriminatory behavior are not tolerated.

## Be Collaborative
Assume good intent, give constructive feedback, and focus on the best outcome.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact maintainers at conduct@hexmon_tech.example.
